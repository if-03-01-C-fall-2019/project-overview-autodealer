Why are we taking this topic?

We took this topic because we are interested in cars at all and the 3 brands are our favourites. That's why we agreed on the three and wanted to create a common website for the brands. We also wanted to compare the cars you like with the ones you like, because it's very practical to see how the cars differ from each other from the brands.
